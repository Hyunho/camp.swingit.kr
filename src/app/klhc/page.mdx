<h1 className='not-prose text-3xl font-bold tracking-tight text-gray-900 sm:text-4xl"'>Korean Lindy Hop Championships </h1>

Korean Lindy Hop Championships(KLHC) is one of the world’s largest international competitions where the best lindy hoppers for each division are selected. We sincerely hope that KLHC does not stop at just being a place to win simple results but we would like it to be a stage of challenge and opportunity. Let that be you under that spotlight.

(Korean Lindy Hop Championships(KLHC)는 각 부문별 최고의 린디 하퍼를 선발하는 세계 최대 규모의 국제 대회입니다. KLHC는 단순히 결과만을 받아들이는 행사에 그치지 않기를 진심으로 바라며, 참가자 분들의 도전과 기회의 무대가 되기를 바랍니다. 참여하는 순간 당신이 주인공입니다.)

## All-Star level entry (M&M and Strictly categories)

1. KLHC (CSI) 
    - Advanced category winners (1st-3rd place)
2. The Battle Korea - Final Showdown (excluding regional battle competitions)
    - Advanced Finalist (1st-2nd place)
    - Allstar M&M Winners (1st-3rd place)
3. ILHC
    - Advanced M&M Category Winners (1st-3rd place)
4. Advanced M&M winners of other domestic and international competitions of the same level or higher (1st to 3rd place)
    - For competitions other than those listed below, please contact the competition management in advance.

- Competitions announced on the ILHC website (High National / International Competitions)
    - ILHC, The Savoy Cup, Camp Hollywood, New Orleans Swing Dance Festival, Boston Tea Party, Camp Jitterbug, Lindy on the Rocks, Camp Swing It, The Snowball, European Swing Dance Championship, The Battle, Balkan Lindy Hop Championships (BLHC), Lindy Shock, or other competitions determined by the organizers.

> Until the 2021 competition, qualifications for participation were separated into M&M and Strictly even at the same Advanced level, but since the All-Star section has been established, from 2023, criteria will be given based on level alone without distinction between the two categories. (Winners in the M&M Advanced category can compete in the Strictly All-star category)


## All-Star 레벨 참가자격 (M&M 및 Strictly 부문)

1. KLHC (CSI)
    - Advanced 부문 입상자(1-3위)
2. The Battle Korea - Final Showdown (지역 배틀 대회 제외)
    - Advanced 결승 진출자(1-2위)
    - Allstar M&M 입상자 (1-3위)
3. ILHC
    - Advanced M&M 부문 입상자 (1-3위)
4. 기타 동급 이상의 국내외 대회 Advanced M&M 입상자 (1-3위)
    - 아래 리스트 이외의 대회인 경우 대회운영진 측에 사전에 문의하시기 바랍니다.
- ILHC 홈페이지에서 안내하는 대회 (High National / International Competitions)
    - ILHC, The Savoy Cup, Camp Hollywood, New Orleans Swing Dance Festival, Boston Tea Party, Camp Jitterbug, Lindy on the Rocks, Camp Swing It, The Snowball, European Swing Dance Championship, The Battle, Balkan Lindy Hop Championships(BLHC), Lindy Shock, or other competitions determined by the organizers.
> 2021년 대회 까지는 같은 Advanced 레벨이라도 M&M과 Strictly로 출전 자격을 분리하였으나, 그동안 올스타 부분이 자리잡은 관계로 2023년 부터는 두개 부문 구별 없이 레벨만으로 기준을 부여합니다. (M&M Advanced 부문 입상자 끼리 Strictly All-star 부분에 출전 가능)
